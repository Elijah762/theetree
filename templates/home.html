Using the past to tell the future

TheeTree alleviates the ambiguity of the diagnosis process and provides transparency in family health history while maintaining anonymity. TheeTree is for families and providers alike to cohesively provide the best care for patients by accounting for diseases within patient lineage, documenting pharmaceutical and medical records, and establishing clear communication between care providers.
